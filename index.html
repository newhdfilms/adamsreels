About

[Your Photo Here]

With more than 20 years in film, TV, and digital media, Adam Litwinski has built a career as a producer, editor, and post-production leader. His past work includes projects for Disney, Marvel, Apple, Amazon, and NBCUniversal. He has directed travel shows throughout Southeast Asia and educational films for U.S. school systems, managed multilingual crews, and led large video-production teams as Director of Video Production at Urban Box Office during the pioneering days of online media.

Over the course of his career, Adam has built and led post-production departments, designing workflows and supervising editors to deliver high-volume, broadcast-ready content under tight deadlines. His background spans both offline creative editorial and senior finishing, allowing him to shape story, pacing, and tone before carrying projects through final delivery for leading studios, networks, and streaming platforms. Today, he continues to evolve modern post workflows by integrating emerging AI tools alongside traditional finishing to support fast, high-quality delivery.
